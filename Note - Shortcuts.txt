By including "QM:\" (without the "quotes") before
a shortcut's command, that shortcut will be
executed as if it was in the QuickMenu\Shortcuts
folder.

  []  Example: QM:\Windows_Explorer.scf would refer
	to the file Windows_Explorer.scf in the
	QuickMenu\Shortcuts folder.
